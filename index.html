Audio Plug-ins

Filterbank band pass eightfold

The Filterbank offers 8 band pass filters to create squelchy, talking bass sounds and evolving pads.

Filterbank is coming soon and will be available in VST, AU, and RTAS formats for Windows and OSX.

Hydra triband crossover

Hydra is a crossover plug-in that splits the audio signal into 3 seperate bands: lows, mids, and highs. These bands are then sent to 3 respective outputs.

Hydra is coming soon and will be available in VST, AU, and RTAS formats for Windows and OSX.
